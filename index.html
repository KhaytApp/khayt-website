Khayt — Production Queue
Everything you need

Built for print shop owners

One app handles your entire workflow — from first quote to final invoice.

Kanban Production Queue

Drag orders across Queued → Printing → Done. See every job at a glance with material, weight, and priority.

Filament Cost Calculator

Price jobs accurately with per-gram filament costs, machine wear, electricity, labor, and failure rates. Save printer presets.

ZATCA-Compliant Invoicing

Generate Phase 1 e-invoices with TLV QR codes. Export as PDF, share via WhatsApp. Built for Saudi market requirements.

Inventory + NFC Spool Scanning

Track filament spools by material, color, and weight. Scan NFC tags to instantly identify and log spool usage.

Analytics & Insights

Revenue trends, top materials, job completion rates, and profit margins — visualized clearly so you can make better decisions.

Client Management

Keep a full history of every client's orders, invoices, and preferences. Build relationships, not just transactions.

Portfolio Gallery

Showcase your best prints with photos, descriptions, and tags. Share your portfolio with potential clients.

Expense Tracking

Log business expenses, categorize costs, and reconcile against revenue so your books stay clean.

Bilingual-first

Built for Arabic makers too

Full Arabic UI with right-to-left layout — not an afterthought, but a core design decision. Switch languages instantly from anywhere in the app.

  • Arabic RTL layout throughout
  • All labels, errors, and dialogs translated
  • ZATCA invoices in Arabic & English
  • Instant language switch, no restart
