Everything you need

Built for print shop owners

One app handles your entire workflow — from first quote to final invoice.

Kanban Production Queue

Drag orders across Pending → Printing → Post-Processing → QC → Done. Multi-machine scheduling, on-hold with reason, and part-level colour assignment.

Smart Cost Calculator

Multi-part cart with live breakdown: material, machine time, electricity, labour, overhead, failure rate & margin. FDM (grams) and Resin (mL) support. G-code / 3MF metadata auto-extraction.

ZATCA-Compliant Invoicing

Phase 1 e-invoices with TLV QR codes, proforma invoices, milestone-based partial billing, quote approval pages, and revision history. PDF export, WhatsApp & email delivery.

Live Printer API

Connect OctoPrint, Moonraker (Klipper), Bambu Lab, PrusaLink, Duet, and Repetier. See real-time temperature and print progress directly from the queue.

Inventory Management

Track FDM spools and Resin bottles with auto-deduction on completion. Spool drying log, test prints, reorder alerts, price history, and overcommit warnings.

Analytics & Break-Even

Revenue, orders, hours, machine P&L, operator performance, retention rates, production heatmap, and cost trends. Break-even dashboard card. NPS survey generator. Simple and Professional reporting modes.

Client CRM & Loyalty

Client profiles with credit limits, bilingual VAT details, loyalty tiers with automatic discounts, intake forms, aged receivables report, and client portal export.

Expenses & Accounting

Recurring expenses (monthly/quarterly/annually), budget tracking, receipt attachments, purchase orders with partial receiving, and double-entry accounting CSV export.

Integrations & Access Control

Outbound HMAC-signed webhooks for order events. Embedded LAN REST API — view queue from any device on your network. Operator PIN lock with Admin / Technician / Sales roles.
